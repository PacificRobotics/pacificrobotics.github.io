Who We Are

Pacific Robotics wants to bring the future to you.

Here at Pacific Robotics, we design autonomous ground vehicles to help you with the dirty work. Futuristic sci-fi films have always depicted the streets filled with robots. There's no reason for a future without robots and there's no reason we are not in the future. We want to bring alive the future, one robot at a time. Tell us what you want to see!

Automation

We believe that the brilliant minds we have can be put to better use elsewhere. That's why we want to take the mundane out of your lives by automation.

Training

You deserve full control over how you would like your job to be automated. This is why we offer training for all the vehicles we build.

Customization

To ensure our robots help you better, we are continuously collecting suggestions to better improve the product you own. We appreciate every single piece of feedback we receive.

Simplification

Good services doesn't have to be expensive or complicated. We make sure that our products can be operated by anyone.

What We Do

We are designing ground vehicles one sector at a time.

Our first venture is to design beach cleaning robots. We want to acknowledge the environmental issues and tackle them as early as we can. For every vehicle purchased, a portion of the profit will be reinvested in providing NGO's with the same technology. Supporting this program can be as easy as telling your friends about us or telling us what your dream robot looks like.

Who

We are a group of engineers and business people who like making robots. Our goal is to build a future with robots that you can get exited about.

What

Out beach cleaning robot will have the capability of autonomously cleaning debris from your beach, as well as making sure your sand is nice and fluffy without rocks.

Where

Currently we are working with beach owners within the province of BC. If you would like to know when we are expanding, please contact us.

When

We would like to have our first product trial in Summer of 2019, beach season! Stay tuned for more details on our product tests.

How

Our device will be a combination of self-driving vehicle technology with traditional sift and rake system tailored for autonomy and safety.
